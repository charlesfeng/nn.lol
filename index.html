Tuesday, March 20, 2018

Ceremony

Starts 9:00 am @ San Francisco City Hall

Our ceremony will be held on Tuesday, March 20, 2018, on the 4th Floor North Gallery in San Francisco City Hall. Come a bit early and park right across the street in the Civic Center Plaza parking structure.

Reception

Bus departs 3:00 pm @ Fairmont Sonoma Mission Inn

Make your way up to sunny Sonoma, about an hour and a half drive north of San Francisco. We’ll meet you in front of the Fairmont Sonoma Mission Inn to welcome you to an adventure through wine country.

Wine tasting 4:00 pm @ Sterling Vineyards

Ride a gondola up to Sterling Vineyards, where we’ll embark on a tour of the estate culminating in a special wine tasting at the South View Terrace with an expansive view of Napa Valley.

Cocktail hour 6:00 pm @ Clos Pegase Winery

Walk across the street to Clos Pegase Winery, internationally acclaimed for its wine and its unique postmodern architecture. Hors d’oeuvres and drinks will be served in the cypress-lined courtyard directly behind the iconic main entrance.

Dinner 7:00 pm @ Clos Pegase Winery

Head into the winery's volcanic knoll and enjoy dinner and toasts underground in the Clos Pegase wine caves.

Open dance floor 8:30 pm @ Clos Pegase Winery

We’ll finish the night with more drinks and dancing under the stars. Our bus will be waiting to take us back to Sonoma at 11:00 pm.
