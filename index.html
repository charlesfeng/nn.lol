Ran & Charles

Tuesday, March 20, 2018

Ceremony

Starts 9:00 am @ San Francisco City Hall

Our ceremony will be held on Tuesday, March 20, 2018, on the 4th Floor North Gallery in San Francisco City Hall. Come a bit early and park right across the street in the Civic Center Plaza parking structure.

Reception

Burgers 12:30 pm @ In-N-Out Burger Novato

Make your way up to sunny Sonoma, about an hour and a half drive north of San Francisco. Grab lunch anywhere you’d like on the way up. We’ve found that delicious burgers are a great way to start a Sonoma wine trip – come join us at In-N-Out Burger!

Bus departs 2:30 pm @ Fairmont Sonoma Mission Inn

We’ll meet you in front of the Fairmont Sonoma Mission Inn to welcome you to an adventure through wine country. Our coach bus will leave from there at 2:30pm sharp.

Wine tasting 3:00 pm @ Balletto Vineyards

We’ll start off with a wine tasting of some of our favorite Pinot Noirs and Zinfandels in Sonoma at Balletto Vineyards. This family-owned, sustainable winery has beautiful grounds and a lovely outdoor patio area where we will hang out until 5pm, when our bus will pick us up again.

Cocktail hour 6:00 pm @ Viansa Winery

Arrive at Viansa Winery, a gorgeous, Tuscan-themed winery on a hilltop with an incredible view of Sonoma Valley. Hors d’oeuvres and drinks will be served in the tree-lined grassy meadow in front of the winery, with full view of the sunset on the valley.

Dinner 7:00 pm @ Viansa Winery

Head to the terrace in front of the tasting room and we’ll have dinner together under the stars.

Open dance floor 8:00 pm @ Viansa Winery

We’ll finish the night with more drinks and dancing in the underground wine cave. Our bus will be waiting to take us back to our hotels at 10:00 pm.

Lodging

San Francisco (night of March 19)

There are many hotels within walking distance of City Hall! The Hayes Valley Inn, the Holiday Inn San Francisco-Civic Center, and the Oak Hotel are all good choices close by.

Sonoma (night of March 20)

The Fairmont Sonoma Mission Inn and Sonoma Creek Inn are just a block away from each other.

Gifts

Your presence is the best gift we can receive – it’s an honor to spend our special day with you. Beyond that, cash or Bitcoin are sincerely appreciated as it helps us visit each other more often!
